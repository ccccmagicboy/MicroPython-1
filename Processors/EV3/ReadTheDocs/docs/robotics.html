robotics – Robotics module¶

Robotics module for the Pybricks API.

class DriveBase(left_motor, right_motor, wheel_diameter, axle_track)¶

Class representing a robotic vehicle with two powered wheels and optional wheel caster(s).

Parameters:
  • left_motor (Motor) – The motor that drives the left wheel.
  • right_motor (Motor) – The motor that drives the right wheel.
  • wheel_diameter (dimension: mm) – Diameter of the wheels.
  • axle_track (dimension: mm) – Distance between the midpoints of the two wheels.
drive(speed, steering)¶

Start driving at the specified speed and turn rate, both measured at the center point between the wheels of the robot.

This method is useful in applications where you often change the speed and steering. For example, when following a line using a light sensor.

Parameters:
  • speed (speed: mm/s) – Speed of the robot. Positive is forward, negative is backward.
  • steering (rotational speed: deg/s) – Turn rate of the robot. Positive is to the right, negative is to the left.
